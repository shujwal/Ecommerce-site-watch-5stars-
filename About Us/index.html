About Us

We are one of the leading e-commerce company in Nepal. We are collaborating with different renowned watch brands around the world like Seiko, Rolex, Casio, Omega, and Zenith. We are providing different watches from thsese brands to our Customers at affordable price with ease. We offer modern, attractive, and durable watches to our Customers. We value your time, we shape your choices, we provide luxurious service on the right time because time is priceless.

Company Information

5 Aces was established in 1999 in Itahari, Sunsari, Nepal. It is the first e-commerce company from Nepal. The founders are Sujal Neupane, Yogesh Phuyal, Deeya Phuyal, Maya Basnet, and Suman Rai. The headquarter of the company is located in Itahari-4, Sunsari. Since its foundation, the company's goal has been to provide watches from different brands at affordable price in convenient way. The company remains committed to this goal. The company's main objective is to address the evolving desires and needs of customers with a very high qualilty service. The performs under the slogan of "Customer satisfaction is our desire."

Mission and Vision

Currently the company has mission to make all the new products available in Nepali market as soon as possible after they launched in global market, while company wants to make better Customer relation too.


In the near future company is going to establish new branches in other cities of Nepal. Company is planning to establish branches in Dharan, Biratnagar, Damak, and in Pokhara. Company is going to add some fashionable watches, smart watches, tech-driven watches, sustainable and eco-friendly watches. Company is doing research about the feasiability of global market too.
